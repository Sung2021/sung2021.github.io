## About_Me
Computational biologist specializing in **target prioritization, multi-omics integration, and interpretable machine learning** for drug discovery. Translates complex multi-modal data into actionable target hypotheses and preclinical decisions — across immune, cardio-metabolic, and neurodegenerative disease areas. Track record of delivering pharmaceutical-grade translational analyses at Battelle and Dana-Farber — including direct BMS collaboration linking clinical treatment response (responder/non-responder) to immune cell dynamics via scRNA-seq/scTCR-seq. Domain experience spans immuno-oncology and metabolic disease (hepatic transcriptomics, high fat diet models).
